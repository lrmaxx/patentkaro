Understanding Patent Protection : Get global patent protection wherever you are

An innovation, a good, or a procedure that offers a novel technical solution is given patent protection. The answer for which patent protection is being sought will not be a readily apparent one; rather, the innovation that is to be protected by a patent must be novel, useful, functional, and original. The technology and composition of new, innovative items are typically subject to patent protection.

Patent protection is granted for an invention, a product, or a process, which brings a new technical solution. The invention, which is to be protected by a patent, must be new, useful, functional, and innovative; that is, the solution for which the patent protection is sought should not be an obvious one.
